The Challenge

Describe the key problems, pain points, or limitations you identified. What made this project challenging? What constraints did you work within?

  • Complex user flows that confused first-time visitors
  • High mobile bounce rate (68%) due to poor mobile experience
  • Outdated visual design that didn't reflect brand values
  • Accessibility issues preventing inclusive access

Research & Discovery

Explain your research methodology. What did you learn about users, competitors, and the market?

User Research

Conducted interviews with 15 target users and analyzed 500+ support tickets to understand pain points. Key findings included:

  • 85% of users preferred visual product browsing over text-heavy pages
  • Users valued quick checkout (under 3 steps)
  • Trust signals (reviews, security badges) significantly influenced purchase decisions

Key Insight: Users were abandoning carts primarily due to unexpected shipping costs appearing late in the checkout process.

The Solution

Describe your design approach and key decisions. What made your solution effective?

Information Architecture

Restructured the site navigation to reduce cognitive load and improve discoverability. Created clear user flows for key tasks.

Visual Design

Developed a modern, clean interface with improved typography hierarchy, strategic use of white space, and a refined color palette that aligns with brand identity.

Interaction Design

Implemented micro-interactions and animations to provide feedback and guide users through key actions. Added progress indicators for multi-step processes.

Implementation & Tools

Discuss the tools and technologies used, and any collaboration with developers or stakeholders.

  • Design: Figma for wireframing, prototyping, and design system
  • Prototyping: Figma, Principle for advanced animations
  • Research: UserTesting, Google Analytics, Hotjar
  • Collaboration: Worked closely with 3 developers and product team

Positive user feedback

The redesign resulted in significant improvements across all key metrics. User satisfaction scores increased from 3.2 to 4.7 out of 5, and the mobile experience saw particular gains with mobile conversions up 58%.

Key Learnings

Reflect on what you learned from this project and how it influenced your approach.

  • Early user testing prevents costly redesigns later
  • Balancing business goals with user needs requires constant communication
  • Accessibility considerations improve the experience for everyone
  • Data-driven decisions lead to better outcomes than assumptions
